# Pure Geometry

This icon is a minimal black-and-white monogram built from two strokes only: a semicircular `C` and a continuous `P`. The background stays pure black, the mark stays pure white, and the composition relies on geometry rather than decoration.

The intent is directness. The `C` reads as an open arc with rounded terminals, while the `P` uses a single uninterrupted stroke to form both stem and bowl. That gives the mark a technical, tool-like quality without adding visual noise.

The result should remain severe and clean: no gradients, no glow, no border, no texture. Just contrast, proportion, and spacing. It is designed to stay legible at small sizes and to work naturally as an app or notification icon.
